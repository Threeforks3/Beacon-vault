# Kickstarter

Beacon's crowdfunding campaign is the public launch vehicle. The campaign serves multiple purposes: funding the first production run, validating market demand, and introducing Beacon to the outdoor and SAR communities.

## Campaign Architecture

**Primary audience:** Paddlers, hikers, backcountry users, SAR volunteers, emergency preparedness buyers.

**Narrative:** "We are looking for you. This helps us find you." — Beacon is designed from the SAR perspective, not the gadget perspective.

**Community integration:** The SAR Coalition provides credibility, endorsement, and distribution. Coalition partners appear in campaign materials. The campaign is a vehicle for introducing the coalition, not just the product.

**Knowledge base:** This vault. Backers and partners can explore the full design rationale — every scenario, every decision, every test result.
