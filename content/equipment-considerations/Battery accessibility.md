# Battery Accessibility

Beacon uses a single CR123A lithium cell — the most common lithium primary battery in emergency equipment. Available worldwide at hardware stores, outdoor retailers, and online. No proprietary battery, no charging infrastructure, no special-ordering.

## Why CR123A

- **Ubiquitous:** Used in cameras, flashlights, tactical equipment, and avalanche transceivers. Available in remote areas where specialty batteries aren't.
- **Lithium chemistry:** -40°C to +60°C operating range. 10-year shelf life. High energy density.
- **Single-cell:** No balancing, no series failure modes. Replace in seconds.
- **Cost:** $2-4 retail, under $1 in volume. Replacement doesn't require a special trip.

## Spares Strategy

A spare CR123A weighs 17g. Two spares triple the runtime for under $10 and 34g. For extended operations (multi-day flood rescue, oceanic search), carrying spares extends the signaling window indefinitely.
