# NASAR

The National Association for Search and Rescue (NASAR) is the U.S. national organization for SAR education, certification, and advocacy. Unlike MRA (team accreditation), NASAR focuses on individual certification and training standards.

## Relevance to Beacon

- **SARTECH certification:** NASAR's individual SAR technician certification program. Certified individuals are potential Beacon evaluators.
- **SAR education:** NASAR's training curriculum covers search theory, navigation, and survival — contexts where signaling devices are directly relevant.
- **Annual conference:** NASAR's conference is a venue for presenting Beacon to the SAR community.

## Current Status

No engagement yet. NASAR is a target organization for coalition outreach.
