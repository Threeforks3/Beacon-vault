# Civil Air Patrol

The Civil Air Patrol (CAP) is the U.S. Air Force auxiliary — a volunteer organization that conducts approximately 85% of inland search and rescue missions in the U.S. CAP operates the largest fleet of single-engine aircraft for SAR in the world.

## Operational Relevance

CAP flies search patterns with Cessna 172 and 182 aircraft — slower and lower than Coast Guard fixed-wing. This is directly relevant to Beacon's visibility from light aircraft at pattern altitude. CAP crews use visual spotting as their primary search technique — an active strobe is the most detectable signal.

## Engagement Status

No current engagement. CAP Florida Wing is a potential coalition partner. The St. Pete Hub is within CAP's operational area.
