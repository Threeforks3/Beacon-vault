# Wilderness Medical Society

The Wilderness Medical Society (WMS) is the leading organization for wilderness medicine — hypothermia, trauma, altitude illness, and prolonged field care in remote settings. WMS publishes practice guidelines used by SAR medics worldwide.

## Relevance to Beacon

WMS guidelines on hypothermia and cold-water immersion directly inform Beacon's cold-weather design requirements. The "golden hour" concept in trauma care translates to Beacon's runtime targets — the device must outlast the medical window.
