# Battery Accessibility

Beacon uses two AAA alkaline cells — the most common battery form factor in the world. Available everywhere: gas stations, supermarkets, hardware stores, airport shops. No proprietary battery, no charging infrastructure, no special-ordering.

## Why 2x AAA

- **Universal:** AAA is the most widely distributed battery size globally. Any country, any retail channel. A stranded paddler can buy replacements at a marina convenience store.
- **Alkaline chemistry:** -20°C to +54°C operating range. Proven in millions of devices. No lithium shipping restrictions.
- **Two-cell series:** Simple, well-understood configuration. Compartments and holders are off-the-shelf.
- **Cost:** $0.50-1 retail per pair, under $0.20 in volume. Replacement is impulse-purchase cheap.
- **Weight:** 23g for the pair. Lighter than a single CR123A.

## Spares Strategy

A spare pair of AAAs weighs 23g and costs under $1. Two spare pairs extend the runtime for under $5 and 46g. For extended operations (multi-day flood rescue, oceanic search), carrying spares extends the signaling window indefinitely.
