# Waterproofing

Beacon is IP68 rated — dust-tight and protected against continuous immersion. The single-body cover with gasket seal means no water ingress even after prolonged submersion to 2 meters.

## IP68 Design

**Sealing:** A single gasket between the cover and the body provides the primary water barrier. The enclosure is sealed at assembly — no user-accessible openings, no battery door gaskets to wear out. Battery replacement requires unscrewing the cover, replacing the cell, and reseating the gasket — a deliberate action, not a daily routine.

**Material:** Polycarbonate cover and body. Polycarbonate is inherently waterproof (non-porous) and dimensionally stable across temperature and humidity ranges. Unlike aluminum (corrodes in salt water) or ABS (absorbs water over time), polycarbonate maintains its seal integrity through repeated immersion cycles.

**Immersions vs spray:** IP68 testing covers continuous immersion at specified depth (2 meters). Actual operational conditions — swift water, rotor wash spray, rain, snow — are less demanding than full immersion. The immersion rating provides margin above operational requirements.

## Salt Water

Sealed electronics with gold-plated contacts resist salt water corrosion. After salt water exposure, the device should be rinsed with fresh water — same protocol as any marine electronics.
