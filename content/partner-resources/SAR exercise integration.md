# SAR Exercise Integration

SAREX (Search and Rescue Exercise) events are the primary venue for introducing new technology to SAR organizations. A SAREX involves multiple agencies — USCG, CAP, MRA teams, local fire/rescue — in a simulated search scenario. New equipment can be evaluated in operational context without the pressure of a real mission.

## Desired Participation

- **Role:** Beacon as evaluated technology — not the subject of the exercise, but a tool available to participants
- **Data collection:** Range tests, activation time, attachment performance, IR visibility through NVG
- **Post-exercise:** Debrief with participating crews. Operational feedback documented in this vault
