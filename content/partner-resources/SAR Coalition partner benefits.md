# SAR Coalition Partner Benefits

Partners in the SAR Guidance Coalition receive Beacon at manufacturing cost, with co-branding, and with full access to the shared knowledge vault. The value proposition is built on transparency, not discounts.

## Core Benefits

**1. Beacon at cost.** Partners pay the verified manufacturing cost — no margin, no markup. Cost is documented and updated as the BOM evolves. Partners may purchase any quantity: evaluation units, team equipment, community distribution. Current target: under $25/unit at 1,000-unit production volume.

**2. Co-branding.** Partner logo laser-etched onto the Beacon cover or strap. "Beacon — in partnership with [SAR Team Name]." The partner's endorsement is visible to their community. Beacon earns credibility. The partner earns a tool their community can afford.

**3. Shared knowledge vault.** This Obsidian vault. Partners can:
- Access all of Beacon's design analysis, test plans, and scenario coverage
- Contribute their own pages — operational protocols, equipment evaluations, case studies
- Link their materials into the existing graph
- See every other partner's contributions

**4. Feedback loop.** Partner operational experience directly shapes Beacon's development. Firmware changes, attachment options, signal patterns — all informed by the people who use it in real rescues.

**5. Replicable template.** Partners who want to form their own regional coalition receive the full framework: partner agreements, vault template, community playbook. The coalition model scales through replication, not centralization.

## What Partners Do Not Receive

- Exclusivity (no territory restrictions)
- Revenue share (Beacon sells through partners, not to them)
- Editorial control (the vault is a shared space, not a marketing channel)
- Obligations (participation is voluntary — no minimum purchase, no required activities)

## Cost Structure

Manufacturing cost target: under $25/unit at 1,000 units. This is the partner price. Retail price target: $49-59. Partners may resell at any price (fundraising, below cost for community service, bundled with training).
