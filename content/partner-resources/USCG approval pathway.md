# USCG Approval Pathway

## Current Status

Beacon is not a carriage requirement — no vessel is required to carry it. It is marketed as supplemental safety equipment. Under CG-ENG Policy Letter 03-18, manufacturers of non-carriage recreational safety equipment may self-certify compliance with applicable standards.

A self-certification bench test plan has been drafted (see Beacon ops/ directory, 2026-06-24).

## Applicable Standards

- **46 CFR 161.013:** Electric distress lights. Establishes performance requirements for brightness, flash rate, waterproofing, and battery life.
- **COLREGs Annex IV:** Distress signals — specifies SOS as the recognized distress pattern.

## Questions for USCG

- Does self-certification require independent lab testing?
- Is there a pathway from self-certified to USCG-recognized?
- Can Beacon participate in SAREX events as an evaluated technology?
