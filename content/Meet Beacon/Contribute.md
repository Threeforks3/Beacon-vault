# Contribute

This compendium is built on source materials — training manuals, field guides, operational doctrine, incident reports. We've identified what we know exists. We haven't found it all yet. If you have access to any of these materials, we'd welcome the contribution.

## Materials We're Looking For

- **COSPAS-SARSAT** — operational specifications, MEOSAR performance data, beacon registration statistics
- **USCG** — remaining training volumes beyond Boat Crew Operations, Coxswain SAR Reference, and Rescue and Survival Procedures
- **Mountain Rescue Association** — field operations guides, search theory manuals, regional protocols
- **Boy Scouts of America** — wilderness first aid curriculum, 10 Essentials field guides, canoeing and kayaking safety
- **American Red Cross** — water safety instructor manuals, wilderness first aid protocols
- **National Park Service** — SAR incident reports, preventive search and rescue program materials
- **Wilderness Medical Society** — clinical practice guidelines for austere environments
- **ICAO** — remaining Annex 12 SAR provisions, regional SAR plans
- **IMO** — International Aeronautical and Maritime SAR Manual (IAMSAR) volumes
- **Civil Air Patrol** — search grid procedures, FLIR operator tactics, mission coordination doctrine

## How to Submit

Email materials, links, or questions to:

**beacon.signal@gmail.com**

If you represent a SAR organization and would like your training materials represented in this compendium, we'll work with you to ensure accurate attribution. Every footnote preserves the source organization's voice verbatim.

## What We Do With Submissions

Source materials are read, cataloged, and analyzed. Key excerpts are preserved with verbatim attribution as coalition footnotes on the relevant scenario pages. Nothing is paraphrased without your review. Nothing is published without your consent.
